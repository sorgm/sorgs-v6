 Various reenactment groups and costume information:
  • Angelcynn Anglo-Saxon Living History -- Interesting articles on crafts in early medieval Europe
  • The Costume Home Page -- one of the most comprehensive listing of costume sites on the web
  • Article on Celtic Brooches
  • BRIGANTIA home page -- group doing Iron-Age Celtic Reenactment in the U.K. -- see their 'kit' page for clothing info.
  • Eachna's Celtic Clothing
  • GALLICA - World of the Celts -- I think the mix Iron Age and Bronze Age, but not bad otherwise...
  • Historical Reenactment (on About.com)
  • House of Tartan - Tartan Finder -- also includes good overview of the history of tartan.
  • Molly NiDana 's Home Page -- an essay on leinte and (soon) on shoes
  • Regia Anglorum Home Page (Anglo-Saxon Living History - good information about contemporary crafts techniques, such as braidwork, embroidery techniques, nalbinding, working with bone and antlers, etc.)
  • Scottish Military Historical Society - Gallery
  • Some Clothing of the Middle Ages -- line drawings of existing bog finds and other surviving garments of the middle ages, including the Moy Bog Dress and the Rogart Shirt.
  • Tartans in GIF Format The Vikings Norse Film and Pageant Society -- See articles on dye colors and stitch types.
  • The Viking Answer Lady's Page -- See her article on how to buy amber without getting cheated.
  • Turm A/S - Viking Jewelry
  • Viking Tunic Construction
