Mary Rose Boot (1549)

 I need to stress that this is guess on my part regarding this boot, based on a very poor quality photograph.  It is a welted shoe, with a rounded toe, and a single outsole.  The vamp appears to fold over and buckle, much like those in the speculative "Thigh high boots"
