Peaked Shoe (c1250-1500)

This shoe, found in  Konstanz, resembles other, similar shoes listed in this work, such as the York Peaked Shoe , the Basic Long Toed Shoe, and the Peaked Shoe .

Has top-bands around the edges of the ankle openings.

This design is based on a description found in Schanck
