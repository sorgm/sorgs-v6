Elizabethan Front-Laced Ladies Shoe

A front-tied shoe, based on a pair described in Arnold Arnold. They are welted shoes with cork soles. The cork being held in a thick leather sole. The shoes are tied by simple laces, drawn though the holes in the latchets and tied.
