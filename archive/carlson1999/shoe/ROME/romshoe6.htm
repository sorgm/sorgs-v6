Roman Shoes

Campagus

  • Campagus/campagi
    Soldiers' shoes, also called campagi militares. In the Edict of Diocletian, these cost 75 denarii.
    • Campagi imperiales
      A shoe, similar to that of the soldiers, worn by emperors in the late empire (e.g., Maximinus and Gallienus) and by Byzantine emperors.
