Hose

Type 2

According to the typology given in Nockert, Type 2 Hose are typified as

"Long hose with straps. The leg cut in one piece -- on the diagonal -- with a rear seam ending in a point at the back. Cut up at the bottom with a triangular piece over the foot, the outer edges of which are wrapped round beneath the foot."

The sole example of this is the Bocksten Man's Other Hose.
