Springhill, Knockan, Parish of Rogart, Sutherland

A number of items were found in a grave near Springhill, Knockan, Parish of Rogart, Sutherland. They may date to the 14th Century, but this is a tentative dating. They consisted of a "shirt", (linen?) stockings, and a shoe (one piece "moccasin", with simple hobnails).
