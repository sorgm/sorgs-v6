Hood

Orkney Hood

This hood from the Orkneys, may date from the "Viking" age. It resembles Roman Cucullus.

Some Sources:

  • Gabra-Sanders, Thea. "An Update on the Orkney Hood." Archaeological Textiles Newsletter.  (Spring 1997)
  • Henshall, Audrey S. "Early Textiles Found in Scotland.  Pt. 1 - Locally Made." Proceedings of the Society of Antiquaries of Scotland (86) 1951-2.
