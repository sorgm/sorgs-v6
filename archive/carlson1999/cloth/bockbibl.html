    It would be impossible for me to express my feelings about the series of books that this tome represents, and this work specifically without sounding like a total suck-up. These people clearly work hard to do the sort of recreation many of us are only able to dream about. I would prefer it if we didn't have to simply rely on an estimate of their scholarship, but were able to actually able to more fully appreciate that scholarship by the publication of their notes. However, there seems to be a point that either you blindly accept the word of the experts, or dig into the information more dilligently. I am pleased to report though, that the material that I've found here and looked to support, has, for the most part, been relatively easy to document.
