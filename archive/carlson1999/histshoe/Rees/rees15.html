Woman's Cork Pump.

The cork is to be prepared. the same as in the shoe, only the edge of it must be taken down thin, and no wider than the inner sole of the pump.ï¿½When you have prepared the inner sole for second lasting, wax the cork to the inner sole, and turn the sock over, which must previously be left, wide enough, and brace it firm over the cork; then wax it over before you put it into the pump.

But mind, when lasting the pump, that you make proper allowance for the cork, otherwise you will have the pump too narrow.
